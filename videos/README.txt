VIDEOS & AUDIO FOLDER
=====================
Place your video and audio files here.

Memory videos (Our Memories → Videos tab):
  memory1.mp4
  memory2.mp4
  memory3.mp4

Video messages from loved ones (Messages → Video/Audio tab):
  msg_mom.mp4        ← video from Mom
  msg_brother.mp4    ← video from brother
  msg_friend.wav     ← audio voice note (also accepts .mp3)

Supported formats:
  Video: .mp4  .webm  .mov
  Audio: .wav  .mp3

After adding your files, update the <source src="..."> paths
in bubbles.html to match your filenames.
